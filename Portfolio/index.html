My Portfolio Some Of My Works

Citrone LMS Redesign: Revolutionizing Learning

The Citrone LMS Redesign project is aimed at revolutionizing the learning experience by enhancing the functionality and user interface of the Citrone Learning Management System (LMS). As a data scientist, my role was to leverage data-driven insights to improve the platform's features and optimize its performance. By implementing advanced analytics and predictive modeling, we aimed to enhance student eligibility predictions, assignment management, and overall user satisfaction.Our goal was to create a seamless and effective learning environment for students and educators, combining data science expertise with user-centric design principles.

Investigating TMDB Movies Data

Conducted a detailed analysis of the TMDB movie dataset to uncover factors associated with high-profitability films. By examining variables such as budget, genre, runtime, and audience ratings, we identified key insights and developed criteria for predicting and maximizing movie profitability. This analysis will empower movie producers, studios, and investors to make data-driven decisions and increase their chances of success in the highly competitive film industry.

Random tweets analysis

These involved collecting and analyzing a large dataset of random tweets to gain insights into trending topics, sentiment analysis, and user behavior. By leveraging natural language processing techniques, valuable information was gotten to understand public opinion, identify emerging trends, and provide actionable insights.

Historical inflation rates

This project involved in-depth analysis and visualization of historical inflation data in Nigeria. By leveraging advanced data visualization techniques, particularly using Power BI,presenting the inflation patterns, trends, and factors contributing to inflation in a comprehensive and visually engaging manner. This provided policymakers, economists, and businesses with valuable insights to make informed decisions and take appropriate actions to mitigate the impact of inflation on the economy.
